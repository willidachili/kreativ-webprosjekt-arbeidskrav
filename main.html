About Superman

Superman origins

Superman was born on the planet Krypton and was given the name Kal-El at birth. As a baby, his parents sent him to Earth in a small spaceship moments before Krypton was destroyed in a natural cataclysm. His ship landed in the American countryside, near the town Smallville. He was found and adopted by farmers Jonathan and Martha Kent, who named him Clark Kent. Clark developed various superhuman abilities, such as incredible strength and impervious skin. His foster parents advised him to use his abilities for the benefit of humanity, and he decided to fight crime as a vigilante. To protect his privacy, he changes into his superhero costume and uses the alias "Superman" when fighting crime. Clark Kent resides in the American city of Metropolis, where he works as a journalist for the Daily Planet.

Powers and abilities

Superman has a wide range of superhuman abilities and powers to aid in his crimefighting. He has superhuman strength, being able to crush coal into diamonds with his bare hands and even shift the orbit of planets. His body is highly durable and invulnerable to almost anything. Bullets bounce harmlessly off his body, and even a nuclear bomb cant stop him. Superman has the ability to fly at great speeds, breaking the sound barrier and in some cases even faster than light to travel to distant galaxies. He can project and percieve X-rays, which allows him to see through objects. His vision can also be amplified to project rays of burning heat, hot enough to melt steel. Superman is able to hear sounds that are too faint for the human ear, and frequencies outside the human hearing range. With these powers combined there's only one man capable of competing with him...

Superman using his heat vision

Archnemesis

Lex Luthor is one of the most dangerously intelligent men on the planet. He is a super-villain, a brilliant scientist, a billionaire industrialist and Superman's greatest enemy. This combination makes him an extremely powerful and formidable opponent; he is ruthless, efficient and creative. In addition to his personal vendetta against the man who thwarts his every scheme, he despises the alien Man of Steel from an ideological standpoint for contradicting his human achievements. He has always been a controversial figure in the public eye due to LexCorp's corrupt business dealings, but he has also maintained political popularity.
